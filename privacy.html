Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 July 2026

Escape in an Envelope (operated by Skilled Forever) makes print-at-home escape-room party games for kids. This policy explains how we handle information for this website and our Pinterest account. In plain terms: we keep it minimal and we don't sell your data.

This website

This site (djsluxx.github.io/escape-in-an-envelope) is a simple, static catalog. It has no accounts, no login, and no contact forms, and we do not run our own analytics or advertising trackers on it. It is hosted on GitHub Pages, whose servers may log standard technical request data (such as IP address and browser type) for security and reliability; this is handled under GitHub's own privacy practices.

Purchases

When you click "Buy," you leave this site and complete your purchase on a third-party marketplace — Etsy or Gumroad. Any information you provide there (name, email, payment, delivery of the digital download) is collected and processed by that marketplace under its own privacy policy, not by us. We only receive the order information the marketplace shares with a seller so we can fulfil and support your purchase.

Our Pinterest account

We use the Pinterest API solely to publish our own product pins to our own Pinterest business account. This is single-account, first-party use. Our app and automation do not access, collect, store, or share any other Pinterest user's personal data, boards, or activity.

What we do not do

  • We don't sell, rent, or trade personal information.
  • We don't build advertising profiles of visitors.
  • We don't knowingly collect personal information from children. Our products are marketed to the parents, teachers, and adults who host the games.

Your choices & contact

For any question about your order or your data, contact us through our Etsy shop: escapeinanenvelop.etsy.com. For data held by Etsy, Gumroad, Pinterest, or GitHub, please also refer to their respective privacy policies.

We may update this policy as the business changes; the "last updated" date above will reflect any revision.
