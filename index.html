Questions

Everything you need to know before the party.

What do I get, and how is it delivered?

An instant PDF download: 6 illustrated clue cards, 7 zone signs, a code card and finale keypad, printable certificates, and a full host guide with the answer key. Nothing is shipped — you print it at home.

Do I need special supplies?

No. Just a home printer (colour recommended) and some tape. No app, no props, no batteries, zero prep.

What ages is it for?

Kits span ages 4–9, each theme labelled by age band. Every puzzle is designed and verified to actually solve at its age.

Can I reuse it?

Yes — print it again for the next birthday, sleepover, rainy day, or classroom.
